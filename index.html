How To Play

The object of the game is to destory the 5 ships on the board:

  • 1 Aircraft Carrier that occupies 5 squares
  • 1 Battleship that occupies 4 squares
  • 1 Cruiser that occupy 3 squares
  • 1 Submarine that occupy 3 squares
  • 1 Destroyer ship that occupies 2 squares

Each ship may occupy spots horizontally or vertically


Scoring works as follows:

  • If you miss a ship: -50
  • If you hit the Destroyer: +1000
  • If you hit the Submarine: +800
  • If you hit the Cruiser: +700
  • If you hit the Battleship: +500
  • If you hit the Aircraft Carrier: +300
